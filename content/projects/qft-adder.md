Implementation of a quantum adder using the Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT), exploring how quantum phase encoding can be used to perform addition on a quantum computer.

Key aspects:
- QFT-based phase encoding of integer values
- Quantum circuit construction for addition using phase kickback
- Simulation and verification using Python/Jupyter notebooks
- Exploration of quantum arithmetic as part of the Quantum Computing coursework at PES University
