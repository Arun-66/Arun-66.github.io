Implementation of standard digital circuits in Cadence Virtuoso, starting from schematic to full custom layout with verification.

Designs implemented:
- Basic gates: AND, NAND, XOR, OR, XNOR, NOR, INV
- Multiplexers: 1:4 and 1:8
- Decoders
- Full Adder, Half Adder, Low Power Full Adder
- DRAM and SRAM cells
- Flip-Flops: JK, SR, T
- 4-bit Inverter
- 4-bit Adder/Subtractor
- 4-bit Ripple Carry Adder

Each design includes schematic capture, simulation, full custom layout, DRC, and LVS sign-off using the Cadence Virtuoso toolchain.
