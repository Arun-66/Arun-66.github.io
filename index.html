Arun Murugappan I

Digital design, hardware verification, and FPGA systems.

NoC Verification Intern · InCore Semiconductors · Bengaluru, Karnataka
About

I'm a final-year Electronics and Communications Engineering student at PES University, graduating in June 2026 with a GPA of 7.93/10. I'm currently a NoC Verification Intern at InCore Semiconductors, where I work on AMBA AHB/APB/AXI4 verification using Embedded UVM in the D language — rewriting golden reference models, scoreboards, sequences, and drivers for scalable, design-independent verification frameworks.

My work spans the full hardware stack: RTL design and verification in SystemVerilog and Verilog, physical design through RTL-to-GDSII flows using Cadence toolchains, and FPGA implementation on Xilinx platforms. Projects have ranged from a complete RV32IM SoC with UART and SPI peripherals to a FIFO taken from RTL to GDSII using the SCL180 PDK, to hardware-software co-design for AES encryption on PYNQ-Z2.

If you're working on digital design, verification, or FPGA systems and want to talk, reach out.
