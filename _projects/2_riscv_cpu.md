Developed an 8-bit RISC-V CPU optimized for low-power IoT applications using energy-efficient design techniques. The design is scalable and tapeout-ready, submitted to the Tiny Tapeout 9 competition by Efabless Corporation.

Performed detailed performance analysis to optimize instruction execution efficiency and minimize power consumption across process, voltage, and temperature corners.

**Tools:** SystemVerilog, C, Assembly
**Year:** 2025
**Award:** Tiny Tapeout Award — Chipalooza ($300), Efabless Corporation
