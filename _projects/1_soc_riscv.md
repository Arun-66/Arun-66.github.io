Designed a complete RISC-V (RV32IM) SoC for FPGA validation, integrating UART, SPI, and QSPI peripheral interfaces. The design targets the SkyWater SKY130A PDK and emphasizes robust peripheral communication and SoC integration.

Applied digital design verification methodologies throughout to ensure functional correctness and evaluate system-level performance. This project represents the most complete end-to-end SoC integration in my portfolio to date.

**Tools:** SystemVerilog, C, Cadence Design Suite, SkyWater SKY130A PDK
**Year:** 2025
