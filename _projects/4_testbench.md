Developed a layered SystemVerilog testbench for a 4-bit shift register, comprising generator, driver, monitor, interface, and scoreboard modules. Applied functional verification methodologies to evaluate performance and identify design issues.

This project was an early exploration of structured verification methodology — the same principles that underpin UVM-based verification flows used in production design.

**Tools:** SystemVerilog
**Year:** 2024
